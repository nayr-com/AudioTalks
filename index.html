Welcome to Audio Talks!

Audio Talks hosts/streams MP3's from different conferences and talks about tech. Use this site when you want something informative to listen to on long walks, train rides, or anywhere that makes talk videos hard to watch.

We try to filter out talks that would difficult to follow without the accompanying video to the best of our ability.

Enjoy!

The Myth of the Genius Programmer

Presenters: Brian Fitzpatrick, Ben Collins-Sussman
An excellent talk given at Google I/O 2009 exploring the idea behind the idea of the genius programmer. Topics also include discussion about open source projects and learning to collaborate effectively.
